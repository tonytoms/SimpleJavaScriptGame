GENERAL RULES



* You will be asked a mathematic question.
* You will be given 4 options out of which one is correct.
* You have 10 seconds to answer each question, after that a 'Time Out' is called.



Level 1



* If you answer correct , the monster will fall back.
* If you answer wrong , the monster will come closer.
* If a 'Time Out' is called , the monster will come closer.
* If the monster goes beyond left screen , you WIN.
* If the monster touches you, you FAIL.



Level 2



* If you answer correct , you will move right.
* If you answer wrong , you will move to 'RED ALERT' area on left.
* If a 'Time Out' is called , you will move to 'RED ALERT' area on left.
* If you go beyond the right screen , you WIN.
* If you enters the 'RED ALERT' area, you FAIL.



Level 3



* enough RUNNING, Its TIME TO KILL THE MONSTER .
* You and the monster has 6 lifes(heart) each shown above each player .
* If you answer correct , the monster will loose his life.
* If you answer wrong , you will loose life.
* If a 'Time Out' is called , you will loose life.
* If the monster looses all lifes before you does, you WIN.
* If you looses all lifes before the monster does, you FAIL.
